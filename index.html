ABOUT US

TACOM (Take A Chance On Me) is an organization with social responsibility based in New York with branches all over the world.

We aim to help children in need, making their Christmas wishes come true with the help of people like you.

We collab with many different organizations like UNICEF in order to receive each children's Christmas Letter and provide them their gifts with the help of charitable people.
